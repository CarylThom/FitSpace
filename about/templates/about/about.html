About Us

In 2015 we decided to make the improvement and maintenance of womens health and wellbeing our mission. FitSpace was created for those seeking to make a life change no matter what your situation. We are a team of trusted personal trainers, nutritionists, physiotherapists, and instructors dedicated to help you begin your journey - Starting with the clothing, equipment and classes we have available here on our website.

Our Goal..

Is to motivate all - No matter your fitness level we will try to help guide you to a happier place. We believe the way to achieve this is with a healthy body, lifestyle and mind. We want to help you build confidence, work together, create friendships with likeminded people, and keep each other motivated while we find your strength.
